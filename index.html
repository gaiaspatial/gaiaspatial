Overview:

Gaia Spatial, LLC is a GIS and Remote Sensing consultancy specializing in integrative geospatial analysis to inform environmental conservation and sustainability research and management. Geospatial analysis fosters integrative perspectives of biological, physical, and social system interrelationships by framing ecological and biophysical theories within site specific, multi-scalar landscape context.
